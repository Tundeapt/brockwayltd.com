Methodology

Built on three principles.

Praxos doesn't guess. Every published pick clears a structural test, a probability floor, and a regime check before it ever appears on the terminal.

01

Poisson distribution engine

A 7×7 scoreline grid evaluated per fixture against current league velocity. Markets graded against actual scoreline mass — not gut feel.

02

Dynamic league velocity

Compression, neutral, and expansion regimes recalibrate every day. A 1-0 league is treated differently from a 3-2 league, automatically.

03

Empirically gated

Each tip has a tracked hit rate over its full sample. Only tips whose per-match confidence clears the long-run floor reach the terminal.
