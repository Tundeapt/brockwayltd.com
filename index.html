FAQ

The honest answers.

Is this a tipster service?
No. Praxos is a quantitative engine. It doesn't issue opinions — it issues markets that have demonstrated proven historical performance under the current match's regime. You see the data behind every pick.
Are the hit rates guaranteed?
No. Hit rates are historical. We never claim 100%. Every pick has a real probability of losing — what we eliminate is selecting markets without a track record.
How is this different from other tip services?
Most services don't show you the success rate of their picks under current match conditions. Praxos surfaces only the top-scoring market per fixture, and shows you the implied minimum odds inline.
