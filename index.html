FAQ

Questions.

Does Shield place trades?
No. Shield has no signal engine, no strategy, no market prediction. It enforces your risk rules. You bring the strategy — Shield makes sure you follow it.
What happens if the server goes down?
Shield is fail-safe, not fail-deadly. Network errors never disable protection. The EA continues enforcing all 8 layers locally for up to 24 hours without server contact. Your positions are never closed due to a network failure.
Can I use Shield on multiple accounts?
One license per MT5 account. The license is portable — if you switch brokers or get a new account, just enter your existing key on the new terminal. No support ticket required.
What is the Guillotine?
Layer 8. If you open a trade without a stop loss, or a trade that exceeds your risk cap, or a trade that would breach your heat cap — Shield closes it within milliseconds of entry. No negotiation.
Will Shield close my trades on license issues?
Never. Shield only closes trades on legitimate risk breaches (floor, guillotine, session, velocity). If your license expires or is revoked, Shield detaches from the chart and leaves all open positions untouched. Your broker connection continues normally.
How do I install Shield?
Drop the .ex5 file into your MT5 Experts folder. Attach to any chart. Enter your license key and Telegram credentials. Shield is live in under 60 seconds.
