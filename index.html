Deployment

Live in four steps.
No code required.

Shield is a compiled MQL5 Execution Terminal. Receive the .ex5 file, drop it in MT5, enter your key.

Apply
Submit your application. Due to the cryptographic locking process, access is currently gated.
Deploy
Once approved, drop the .ex5 into your MT5 Experts folder and restart the terminal.
Configure
Attach to chart. Enter your unique License Key. The custom trading panel replaces native MT5 controls immediately.
Enforced
Draw your SL. Execute through the panel. Shield handles the sizing, constraints, and session sweeps automatically.
