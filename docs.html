Installation

STEP 01
Receive your license key

After payment confirmation via Korapay, you will receive an email from Brockway containing your license key and the Shield EA file (Shield_v2.ex5). The key is bound to the MT5 account ID you provided at checkout.

STEP 02
Install the EA in MetaTrader 5
  1. Open MT5. Go to File → Open Data Folder
  2. Navigate to MQL5/Experts/
  3. Copy Shield_v2.ex5 into this folder
  4. Restart MT5 or right-click Experts panel → Refresh
STEP 03
Whitelist the WebRequest URLs

Shield requires network access to post telemetry. In MT5:

  1. Go to Tools → Options → Expert Advisors
  2. Check Allow WebRequest for listed URL
  3. Add: https://shield-api-production-8317.up.railway.app
  4. Add: https://api.telegram.org (for Telegram alerts)
  5. Click OK
STEP 04
Attach Shield to a chart

Drag Shield_v2 from the Navigator panel onto any chart. In the inputs panel, enter:

  • InpLicenseKey — your license key from the email
  • InpDailyFloor — your prop firm's daily loss limit (e.g. -4.0 for FTMO)
  • InpMaxDrawdown — your prop firm's max drawdown (e.g. -8.0)
  • All other inputs: leave at defaults (Ratchet, Velocity, News, Alerts all ON)

Enable AutoTrading in the top toolbar. Shield is now armed.

STEP 05
Verify telemetry is live

Open your dashboard at brockwayltd.com and enter your license key. You should see your account appear with live equity, balance, and risk state within 10 seconds.

The Eight Layers of Enforcement

Shield enforces eight distinct protection layers simultaneously. Any one of these can stop a trade or close a position.

01 · FLOOR
Hard daily loss limit. When breached, Shield closes all positions and locks the account until next trading day.
02 · RATCHET
Automatic gain lock-in. As equity grows through defined tiers, the floor ratchets upward to protect realized gains.
03 · TRAIL
Dynamic floor that tracks the high water mark, protecting equity from round-trips below peak.
04 · HEAT CAP
Limits total open exposure across all positions. Prevents over-leveraging into a single setup.
05 · SESSION
Hard close at 21:55 UTC in Prop Strict profile. No overnight exposure. No override. Protects from weekend gaps.
06 · NEWS FILTER
Blocks new positions during high-impact news windows. Prevents execution during volatility spikes.
07 · VELOCITY HALT
Detects rapid drawdown (0.5% / 5 minutes) and halts trading temporarily. Flash crash protection.
08 · GUILLOTINE
Reactive enforcement via OnTradeTransaction. Closes any position opened without a stop loss, exceeding per-trade risk, or breaching the heat cap — within milliseconds of entry.

Troubleshooting

Telemetry not reaching the dashboard

Verify the Railway URL is whitelisted in Tools → Options → Expert Advisors. Check the Experts log for SHIELD DASH | Error messages. Silent when healthy is normal behavior.

Telegram alerts not arriving

Ensure https://api.telegram.org is whitelisted. Confirm your bot token and chat ID are set correctly in the EA inputs. Test by forcing a trigger (e.g. breach a test floor on a demo account).

License key rejected

License keys are bound to the MT5 account ID you provided at checkout. If you changed accounts or brokers, contact support to rebind. Do not share license keys — rebinding is free within the same subscription.
